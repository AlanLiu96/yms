Your Meal Swipe
===

A meteor application for Yale students to plan out their $8.00 of meal swipe credit

<h3>Local run:</h3>

<ol>
<li>Run meteor.exe</li>
<li>Point browser to <a href='http://localhost:3000'>localhost:3000</a> </li>
</ol>
